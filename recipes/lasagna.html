Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a standard meat lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook noodle
  2. Cook Beef
  3. Add tomato sauce to beef>
  4. Put down a layer of noodles
  5. Add tomato sauce with beef
  6. Ad shredded cheese
  7. Build more noodle layers until out of noodles
